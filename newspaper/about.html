About us

Welcome to PJ Markets, your number one source for all things advertising. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of advertising, with a focus on customer service, reliability, low prices for quality.

Founded in 2020 by the CEO, PJ Markets has come a long way from its beginnings in Cape Town. When the CEO first started out, their passion for eco-friendly cleaning products drove them to quit day job, do tons of research, so that PJ Markets can offer you "the world's most advanced newspaper. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
